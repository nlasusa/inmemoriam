In Loving Memory of
Kathy L. Vander Veur
Kathy L. Vander Veur was a cherished and dedicated member of the Kessler Topaz family for over two decades. She passed away on July 8, 2019, following a courageous battle with cancer. During her time with the Firm, Kathy served in many important administrative and creative roles, always bringing warmth and a deep sense of loyalty. She cared deeply about the Firm and its success, embodying a quiet strength that left a lasting impact. Kathy was a kind and empathetic soul with a special love for animals—especially horses—and a deep affection for her cat, Frodo. True to her private nature, she kept her illness and pain to herself, never seeking sympathy or wanting to be treated differently. Kathy will be dearly missed by her family, friends, and the many colleagues whose lives she touched.

1954 - 2019
In Loving Memory of
Johanna M. Conicello
Johanna M. Conicello was a beloved and highly respected member of the Kessler Topaz family for over 15 years. She passed away on January 22, 2025, following a valiant battle with cancer, leaving behind a legacy of dedication, grace, and strength. Joining the firm in 2010, Johanna served as an exceptional Paralegal Manager. Her expertise, leadership, and tireless commitment had a lasting impact on both our work and our people. Always ready to support colleagues, mentor others, and lead by example, her calm presence and warm spirit fostered a strong sense of community. Beyond her professional contributions, Johanna was a beloved colleague and friend. Her kindness and unwavering support touched everyone who had the privilege of knowing her. Johanna will be deeply missed by her family, friends, and all of us at KTMC.

1980 - 2025
